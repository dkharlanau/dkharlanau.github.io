<section class="section atlas-hero">
  <p class="eyebrow">Skill Hub — SAP AMS / Operations</p>
  <h1>Practical skills for SAP operations and support.</h1>
  <p class="lead">Methods for triaging incidents, finding root causes, assessing change impact, capturing operational knowledge, and stopping recurring tickets. Designed for SAP AMS consultants and applicable to any ERP operations context.</p>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>What this skill group covers</h2>
  </header>
  <p>This group covers the core operational skills needed to keep SAP systems stable, resolve incidents efficiently, and prevent the same problems from repeating. The skills are designed for consultants working in AMS, support, or operations roles who need structured methods rather than ad-hoc troubleshooting.</p>
  <p>Each skill is a complete working method: questions to ask, steps to follow, decision rules to apply, and artifacts to produce.</p>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>Skills in this group</h2>
  </header>
  <div class="topic-grid">
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/sap-ams/incident-triage-working-skill/">Incident Triage</a></h3>
      <p>Classify incidents fast, contain business impact, and route to the right owner without wasting time on premature diagnosis.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/sap-ams/root-cause-analysis-working-skill/">Root Cause Analysis</a></h3>
      <p>Separate symptoms from causes, trace defects to their entry point, and produce a correction and prevention plan.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/sap-ams/change-impact-analysis-working-skill/">Change Impact Analysis</a></h3>
      <p>Before applying a transport, configuration change, or master data update, know which processes, interfaces, and stakeholders are affected.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/sap-ams/operational-knowledge-capture-working-skill/">Operational Knowledge Capture</a></h3>
      <p>Turn firefighting into reusable knowledge: capture what was done, why it worked, and when it applies.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/sap-ams/recurring-ticket-pattern-analysis-working-skill/">Recurring Ticket Pattern Analysis</a></h3>
      <p>Find the patterns behind repeated tickets, quantify the cost, and design a permanent fix instead of handling the same symptom forever.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>When to use this group</h2>
  </header>
  <ul>
    <li>You are joining an AMS team and need structured methods instead of tribal knowledge.</li>
    <li>Incidents are taking too long to resolve because ownership and scope are unclear.</li>
    <li>The same tickets appear every week and no one has time to find the pattern.</li>
    <li>Changes are going live without understanding downstream impact on interfaces or processes.</li>
    <li>Key team members are leaving and their troubleshooting knowledge is not documented.</li>
    <li>You need to train an AI agent to produce useful AMS artifacts instead of generic summaries.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>How these skills fit together</h2>
  </header>
  <ol>
    <li><strong>Incident Triage</strong> is the entry point: classify and route fast.</li>
    <li><strong>Root Cause Analysis</strong> follows when the same incident type repeats or when a high-impact incident needs permanent resolution.</li>
    <li><strong>Change Impact Analysis</strong> prevents incidents by checking what a change will break before it goes live.</li>
    <li><strong>Operational Knowledge Capture</strong> records what the team learns during triage and root cause work.</li>
    <li><strong>Recurring Ticket Pattern Analysis</strong> connects the dots across many incidents to find systemic failures.</li>
  </ol>
</section>

  </header>
