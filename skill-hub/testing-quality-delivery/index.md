  <h1>Validate delivery before it reaches production.</h1>
  <p class="lead">Skills for designing test cases, planning regression tests, triaging defects, running QA reviews, preparing deployment readiness checks, organizing UAT, reviewing test evidence, and assessing release risk. Every skill produces a concrete artifact that reduces the chance of production failure.</p>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>What this group covers</h2>
  </header>
  <p>Testing, QA, and Delivery Validation skills help you catch defects before they reach users, prove that changes work as intended, and document that proof so auditors and stakeholders can trust it. These skills cover the full validation lifecycle: from writing the first test case to signing off the release.</p>
  <p>These skills are cross-domain. They apply to SAP implementations, integration projects, data migrations, custom development, and packaged software deployments. They are designed to be used together: test case design feeds regression planning, regression planning feeds UAT preparation, and UAT preparation feeds the QA sign-off that gates release.</p>
  <p>Each skill page includes: a working method, diagnostic questions, decision rules, artifact templates, quality checklists, and specific instructions for AI agents.</p>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>When to use this group</h2>
  </header>
  <ul>
    <li>A change is ready for testing and no one has written the test cases yet.</li>
    <li>A new feature or fix requires regression testing and the team needs to decide what to retest.</li>
    <li>Defects are arriving from test, UAT, or production and the team needs to classify, prioritize, and route them.</li>
    <li>A release is scheduled and the QA team must verify readiness before approving deployment.</li>
    <li>UAT is about to start and the business testers need preparation, data, and clear instructions.</li>
    <li>Test execution has finished and the evidence must be reviewed for completeness, accuracy, and coverage.</li>
    <li>A steering committee or CAB must decide whether a release is safe to deploy based on risk evidence.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>Skills in this group</h2>
  </header>
  <div class="topic-grid">
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/testing-quality-delivery/test-case-design-working-skill/">Test Case Design</a></h3>
      <p>Write step-by-step test cases that verify requirements, cover edge cases, and can be executed by testers who do not know the system internals.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/testing-quality-delivery/regression-test-planning-working-skill/">Regression Test Planning</a></h3>
      <p>Select what to retest based on change risk, impact area, and historical defect density so regression effort is focused and defensible.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/testing-quality-delivery/defect-triage-classification-working-skill/">Defect Triage and Classification</a></h3>
      <p>Classify incoming defects by severity, priority, root cause area, and owner so the right team fixes the right defects in the right order.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/testing-quality-delivery/qa-review-sign-off-working-skill/">QA Review and Sign-Off</a></h3>
      <p>Run a structured quality gate review before release: verify test coverage, defect status, documentation completeness, and risk acceptance.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/testing-quality-delivery/deployment-readiness-checklist-working-skill/">Deployment Readiness Checklist</a></h3>
      <p>Confirm that every prerequisite for safe deployment is complete: tests passed, rollback plan ready, stakeholders informed, monitoring active.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/testing-quality-delivery/uat-preparation-working-skill/">UAT Preparation</a></h3>
      <p>Prepare business testers with clear scenarios, test data, environment access, and defect reporting rules so UAT produces valid sign-off.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/testing-quality-delivery/test-evidence-review-working-skill/">Test Evidence Review</a></h3>
      <p>Review executed test evidence for completeness, accuracy, and traceability so the test record is audit-ready and credible.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/testing-quality-delivery/release-risk-review-working-skill/">Release Risk Review</a></h3>
      <p>Assess release risk by weighing open defects, untested areas, dependency changes, and business impact so the go/no-go decision is evidence-based.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
