<section class="section atlas-hero">
  <p class="eyebrow">Skill Hub — Decision & Validation</p>
  <h1>Make evidence-based decisions and validate requirements before they become expensive defects.</h1>
  <p class="lead">Skills for evaluating alternatives, mapping risks and dependencies, building evidence-based recommendations, reviewing requirements for quality, tracing requirements through design and test, and deriving concrete test scenarios. Usable on enterprise, SAP, and integration projects where every decision must be defensible and every requirement must be verifiable.</p>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>What this group covers</h2>
  </header>
  <p>Decision & Validation skills help you evaluate options and validate work products before they enter implementation. They provide structured methods for comparing alternatives, assessing risk and dependency, building recommendations with evidence, reviewing requirements for completeness and consistency, tracing requirements from origin to verification, and converting requirements into testable scenarios.</p>
  <p>These skills are cross-domain. They apply to SAP implementations, integration projects, data governance initiatives, architecture decisions, and operational improvement. They are designed to be used together: a decision without validation is a guess, and validation without a clear decision context is waste.</p>
  <p>Each skill page includes: a working method, diagnostic questions, decision rules, artifact templates, quality checklists, and specific instructions for AI agents.</p>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>When to use this group</h2>
  </header>
  <ul>
    <li>A project must choose between multiple solutions and needs a structured comparison method.</li>
    <li>A recommendation must be made to stakeholders and needs evidence, not opinion.</li>
    <li>Risks and dependencies are unclear and must be mapped before a decision is committed.</li>
    <li>Requirements are drafted and need formal review before they are approved for build.</li>
    <li>Requirements are approved and must be traced through design, test, and release to prove coverage.</li>
    <li>Test planning starts and concrete scenarios must be derived from requirements and acceptance criteria.</li>
    <li>An audit or compliance review asks for evidence that every requirement was reviewed, tested, and released.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section class="section">
  <header class="section-heading">
    <h2>Skills in this group</h2>
  </header>
  <div class="topic-grid">
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/decision-validation/trade-off-analysis-working-skill/">Trade-off Analysis</a></h3>
      <p>Evaluate competing objectives by identifying what must be sacrificed, what must be protected, and what criteria make the decision defensible.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/decision-validation/option-comparison-matrix-working-skill/">Option Comparison Matrix</a></h3>
      <p>Compare alternatives side-by-side using weighted criteria so that the best option is visible and the rationale is auditable.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/decision-validation/risk-dependency-mapping-working-skill/">Risk Dependency Mapping</a></h3>
      <p>Map what depends on what, identify single points of failure, and expose hidden assumptions before they become blockers.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/decision-validation/evidence-based-recommendation-working-skill/">Evidence-Based Recommendation</a></h3>
      <p>Build recommendations that are supported by data, traceable to sources, and structured for stakeholder decision-making.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/decision-validation/requirements-review-checklist-working-skill/">Requirements Review Checklist</a></h3>
      <p>Review requirements for completeness, consistency, testability, and feasibility before they are approved for implementation.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/decision-validation/traceability-matrix-working-skill/">Traceability Matrix</a></h3>
      <p>Map requirements to source, design, test, and release so that every requirement can be traced from origin to verification.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="topic-card">
      <h3><a href="/skill-hub/decision-validation/test-scenario-derivation-working-skill/">Test Scenario Derivation</a></h3>
      <p>Derive concrete test scenarios from requirements and acceptance criteria so that testers know exactly what to verify.</p>
    </div>
