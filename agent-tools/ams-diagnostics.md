<section class="section note-detail"><p class="eyebrow">AMS</p><h1>MCP for SAP AMS and diagnostics</h1><p class="lead">Good incident support begins with evidence and a clear uncertainty boundary, not a live-system agent connection.</p><div class="note-body"><p><a href="/agent-tools/sap-diagnostics-mcp/">SAP Diagnostics MCP</a> stays at Level 0: it searches reviewed Atlas content and builds structured briefs without connecting to SAP. Use it to form a checklist and separate facts from hypotheses before deciding whether a read-only system check is needed.</p><p>For a status-51 issue, capture the IDoc number, direction, partner, message type, status history, error text, timestamps, and affected volume. The agent may organize the evidence; a responsible support owner confirms the cause and authorizes any reprocessing.</p></div></section>
