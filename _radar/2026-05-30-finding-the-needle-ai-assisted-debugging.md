SAP Architecture Center reports on using AI assistants (Claude) to trace a hidden credential bug through thousands of lines of cloud API logs in under 60 minutes.

**Key points:**

- AI-assisted debugging can process large log volumes faster than manual grep/trace cycles
- The specific case involved a credential propagation bug in a cloud integration landscape
- Claude was used to analyze logs, trace the failure chain, and identify the root cause

**Practical impact:**

For AMS teams managing complex integration landscapes, this is a concrete use case for AI-assisted troubleshooting. The speed claim (under 60 minutes) is significant if reproducible, but the article is a case study rather than a controlled benchmark. Teams should treat this as a signal to experiment with AI tools for log analysis, not as a proven productivity multiplier.
