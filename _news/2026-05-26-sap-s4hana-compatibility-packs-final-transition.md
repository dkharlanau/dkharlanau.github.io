SAP is completing the transition away from compatibility packs in S/4HANA Cloud.

**Key points:**

- Compatibility packs are being replaced by scope items and extensibility options
- Clean Core strategy requires standard processes where possible
- Migration timeline depends on the specific pack and release

**Practical impact:**

For teams still using compatibility packs, the priority is mapping current processes to standard scope items or planning extensibility alternatives. The earlier this mapping starts, the smoother the transition.
