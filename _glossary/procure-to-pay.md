    <h1>Procure to Pay (P2P)</h1>
    <div class="atlas-pill-row">{% include atlas/status-badge.html %}</div>
  </header>

  <div class="note-body">
    <p><strong>Procure to Pay (P2P)</strong> is the process chain from identifying a need for goods or services through requisition, purchase order, goods receipt, invoice verification, and final payment to the vendor.</p>

    <h2>Why it matters</h2>
    <p>P2P touches procurement, inventory, finance, and vendor master data. Master-data inconsistencies or approval bottlenecks often produce repeated support incidents.</p>
