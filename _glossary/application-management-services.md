    <h1>Application Management Services (AMS)</h1>
    <div class="atlas-pill-row">{% include atlas/status-badge.html %}</div>
  </header>

  <div class="note-body">
    <p><strong>Application Management Services (AMS)</strong> is the ongoing support, maintenance, and continuous improvement of business applications after they go live. For SAP, AMS covers incident resolution, change requests, monitoring, patching, release management, and knowledge handover.</p>

    <h2>Why it matters</h2>
    <p>AMS is where operational memory is built or lost. Stable support depends on clean documentation, repeatable diagnostics, and controlled change processes.</p>
