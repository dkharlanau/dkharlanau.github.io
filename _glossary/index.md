    <p class="note-subtitle">Concise, public-safe definitions of SAP, AMS, and AI concepts used across the site.</p>

  <div class="note-body">
    <p>This glossary is a review candidate. New terms are added with <code>noindex</code> until they pass human review.</p>

    <h2>Terms</h2>
    <dl class="glossary-term-list">
      <dt><a href="/atlas/glossary/order-to-cash/">Order to Cash (O2C)</a></dt>
      <dd>The end-to-end flow from customer order to delivery, billing, and cash collection.</dd>

      <dt><a href="/atlas/glossary/procure-to-pay/">Procure to Pay (P2P)</a></dt>
      <dd>The process chain from procurement need to purchase order, goods receipt, invoice verification, and payment.</dd>

      <dt><a href="/atlas/glossary/available-to-promise/">Available to Promise (ATP)</a></dt>
      <dd>A promise logic that checks whether requested quantities can be promised to a customer at a given date.</dd>

      <dt><a href="/atlas/glossary/application-management-services/">Application Management Services (AMS)</a></dt>
      <dd>Ongoing support, maintenance, and improvement of business applications such as SAP after go-live.</dd>

      <dt><a href="/atlas/glossary/business-partner/">Business Partner (BP)</a></dt>
      <dd>The central SAP S/4HANA master-data object that can represent customers, vendors, and other business relationships.</dd>

      <dt><a href="/atlas/glossary/clean-core/">Clean Core</a></dt>
      <dd>An SAP strategy to keep the ERP core stable, upgraded, and free of custom code that blocks cloud innovation.</dd>

      <dt><a href="/atlas/glossary/side-by-side-extensibility/">Side-by-Side Extensibility</a></dt>
      <dd>Extending SAP functionality through external services and apps rather than modifications inside the core.</dd>

      <dt><a href="/atlas/glossary/operational-memory/">Operational Memory</a></dt>
      <dd>The captured, searchable record of how a system, process, or organization actually behaves and is supported.</dd>
    </dl>
