    <h1>Clean Core</h1>
    <div class="atlas-pill-row">{% include atlas/status-badge.html %}</div>
  </header>

  <div class="note-body">
    <p><strong>Clean Core</strong> is SAP's strategy to keep the ERP core stable, upgradable, and free of modifications that block cloud innovation. It favors standard processes, released APIs, and extensions built outside the core.</p>

    <h2>Why it matters</h2>
    <p>A clean core reduces upgrade risk and makes side-by-side extensions, AI agents, and cloud integrations easier to adopt and maintain.</p>
