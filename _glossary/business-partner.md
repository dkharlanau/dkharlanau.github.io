    <h1>Business Partner (BP)</h1>
    <div class="atlas-pill-row">{% include atlas/status-badge.html %}</div>
  </header>

  <div class="note-body">
    <p><strong>Business Partner (BP)</strong> is the central master-data object in SAP S/4HANA that represents any entity the company has a relationship with. A single business partner can have multiple roles, such as customer, vendor, or contact person.</p>

    <h2>Why it matters</h2>
    <p>BP unifies customer and vendor master data. Replication, role assignment, and number-range issues are common sources of integration and support tickets.</p>
