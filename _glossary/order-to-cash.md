    <h1>Order to Cash (O2C)</h1>
    <div class="atlas-pill-row">{% include atlas/status-badge.html %}</div>
  </header>

  <div class="note-body">
    <p><strong>Order to Cash (O2C)</strong> is the end-to-end business process that turns a customer order into delivered goods or services, an invoice, and collected cash. In SAP it typically spans sales order creation, availability checks, delivery, goods issue, billing, accounting, and receivables management.</p>

    <h2>Why it matters</h2>
    <p>O2C is where customer promise, logistics execution, pricing, credit, and finance intersect. Friction in one step usually appears as a support ticket in another.</p>
