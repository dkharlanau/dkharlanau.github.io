    <h1>Operational Memory</h1>
    <div class="atlas-pill-row">{% include atlas/status-badge.html %}</div>
  </header>

  <div class="note-body">
    <p><strong>Operational Memory</strong> is the captured, searchable record of how a system, process, or support organization actually works. It includes runbooks, incident patterns, decisions, known errors, and handover notes that let teams recover context quickly.</p>

    <h2>Why it matters</h2>
    <p>Without operational memory, support teams repeat diagnoses and lose knowledge when people rotate. Good operational memory is the foundation of effective AMS and AI-assisted support.</p>
