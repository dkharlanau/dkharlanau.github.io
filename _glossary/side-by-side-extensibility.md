    <h1>Side-by-Side Extensibility</h1>
    <div class="atlas-pill-row">{% include atlas/status-badge.html %}</div>
  </header>

  <div class="note-body">
    <p><strong>Side-by-Side Extensibility</strong> is the practice of extending SAP functionality through external applications, services, and integrations rather than modifying code inside the ERP core. It is a key enabler of the clean-core strategy.</p>

    <h2>Why it matters</h2>
    <p>Side-by-side extensions can be upgraded, replaced, and scaled independently of the core ERP. They rely on stable APIs and clear data contracts.</p>
