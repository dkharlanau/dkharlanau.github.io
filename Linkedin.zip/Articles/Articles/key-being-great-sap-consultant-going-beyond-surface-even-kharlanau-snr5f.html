The Key to Being a Great SAP Consultant: Going Beyond the Surface (Even When It's Tough)

Created on 2024-10-01 13:47

Published on 2024-10-01 18:15

From what I’ve seen, the best SAP consultants don’t just scratch the surface—they have a deep understanding of both processes and systems. It’s not just about following steps or applying quick fixes; it’s about understanding why things work and how they connect. But getting to that level isn’t easy—it’s hard, and it takes commitment.

What Sets the Best Apart

The best consultants I’ve worked with always dig deeper. They don’t just solve a problem; they figure out why it’s happening and how it fits into the bigger picture. This doesn’t come from manuals or shortcuts—it comes from experience. These people have been through so much that they just know how to navigate SAP.

It’s Hard, But Necessary

Here’s the truth: it’s tough to push yourself to develop that kind of understanding. Your brain doesn’t want to—it wants the easy way out. But to really be great, you need to bypass that instinct and commit to learning the system deeply. It’s not always fun, but it pays off.

My Technique for Going Deep

  1. Don’t jump straight into the task. I always ask myself how this issue fits into the larger process. It helps me make smarter decisions.

  2. Enhancements are learning opportunities. I look at how changes affect the system as a whole. Yes, it’s more work, but that’s where the deep understanding comes in.

  3. Challenges suck, but they’re where the learning happens. I take the time to understand why things went wrong, not just fix them.

  4. I always write down key takeaways after a project. It reinforces what I’ve learned and helps me improve for next time.

  5. I like to dig into how things work, even when there’s no problem to solve. Curiosity keeps me sharp and ready for the next challenge.

The Brain Wants Easy, But You Have to Push. It’s easy to fall into the trap of doing just enough to get by. But if you push through the resistance and commit to understanding SAP at a deeper level, you’ll solve problems faster and deliver better solutions.
