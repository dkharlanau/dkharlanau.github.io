When it comes to creating engaging and functional SAP reports, ALV (ABAP List Viewer) is one of the most powerful tools at your disposal. From normal table displays to intricate hierarchical representations, ALV ensures data is presented effectively.

Below, I'll walk you through key SALV features, hierarchical table handling, and some advanced tricks with examples to make your reports not just functional but impressive.
