Ever stared at a massive to-do list and thought, Where do I even start? That’s where the Effort/Impact Framework comes in. It’s a simple tool to help you focus on tasks that give the best bang for your buck.

What Is It?

The Effort/Impact Framework maps tasks based on:

  • Impact: How much value will it bring?

  • Effort: How hard will it be to complete?

The result? A 2x2 matrix that guides your decisions:

How to Use It

  1. List Tasks: Brainstorm everything you need to do. For SAP consulting, think enhancements, bug fixes, or MRP configurations.

  2. Score Each Task: Rate impact and effort on a simple scale (e.g., 1 to 10).

  3. Plot on the Matrix: Place tasks in one of the four quadrants.

  4. Prioritize

What to Do: Knock out the quick wins (bug fix, MRP handling), then focus on the custom ATP logic. Skip the purchase order cleanup unless forced.

Why It Works

  • Simple: No complex tools needed.

  • Focused: Eliminates decision fatigue.

  • Clear: Makes priorities easy to communicate.
